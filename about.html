Community Impact

Charles Ellington, father of Rob Ellington, has had a large impact on the community of Victoria. From his humble upbringing in an orphanage he worked hard to earn a successful career as a Notary Public. Not resting on the laurels of his success, Charles gave back to the community in a number if philanthropical endeavours. Charles would spend 25 years on the board of the British Columbia Prostestant Orphans Home, including 5 years as board chair. From his time there he became a founding chair of the board of the Cridge Centre for the Family. Charles aided in the transition of the Centre from the now outdated role of an Orphanage to a facility designed for the housing and service of single parents. This included child day care and in time housing for senior citizens.
