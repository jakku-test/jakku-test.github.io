Services

At Ellington & Ellington Notaries Public, we like to make our clients feel comfortable. You can sit down, explain your case and leave the rest to us. Many of our clients don’t realize the many extra services we’re able to offer as notaries public in the British Columbia province. Only in British Columbia and Quebec are notaries permitted to dispense legal advice, handle real estate law and prepare wills and powers of attorney, for instance.
