Services

Real Estate

A Real Estate transaction is a major life event. Relieve some of the burdon by consulting with an expert who deals with the task on a daily basis. Let us set your mind at ease over one of the biggest financial decisions you will make in your life.

Powers Of Attorney

Drafting a Power of Attorney is an important procedure. To entrust someone with the authority to act on your behalf should you not be able to is an important thing. To go over the complexities that come along with a Power of Attorney is something we can help with.

Will & Estate Planning

In our personal and professional experience it is important to be prepared for the unexpected realities of life. Drafting a Will or planning your Estate's future may allow you to live in the present rather than be caught worrying about the uncertain future.

Affidavits

Looking for help with the Notarization of an Affidavit? As a licensed Notary Public in British Columbia, we can help. Having a legal expert Notarize your Affidavit is important.

Mortgages

We are able to help when it comes to understanding the fine print of a Mortgage and what it entails. However you aquire your mortgage be it Bank or Broker, you want to be able to understand the legalities. Our experience in the field can help simplify it.

Notarizations

Need a document Notarized by a licensed Notary Public? For anything from Affidavits, Sworn statements, to Travel letters and more. A professional notarization can help save you from a future headache.
