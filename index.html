Why Use A Notary?

Whether it's Real Estate Transfers, Mortgages, Powers of Attorney, Wills & Estates, Affidavits, Notarizations, or any other number of actions, it's important that you know when and why a Notary Public is important for you in legal situations. In the province of British Columbia, Notaries Public exist to help you with legal services such as those listed above.
