Why Use A Notary?

Whether it's Real Estate Transfers, Mortgages, Powers of Attorney, Wills & Estates, Affidavits, or Notarizations, it's important that you know when and why a Notary Public should be useful to you. In the province of British Columbia, Notaries Public exist as an impartial third party to help you with legal services such as those listed above. If the answer to your question has not been addressed here, get in touch with us by phone or email to find out how we can help you.
